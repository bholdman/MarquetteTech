Codefi

Codefi opened its doors in the historic Federal Building in November, 2014. As the area’s first co-working space and technology incubator, Codefi quickly became a catalyst for talent attraction, education, technology development, and startup investment. Now calling the Marquette Tower home with a 19,000+ square-foot facility, Codefi anchors the Tech District to support more startups and tech-based businesses in the area’s most tech-powered and modern business center.

Codefi provides startup entrepreneurs and emerging innovative companies with tech-powered, flexible enterprise space, while serving the area with quality programming and an inspiring community. Whether you need private offices with hundreds of square feet or just a chair and desk, we have it. More than just beautiful workspaces—we’re a community combining our collective talents to create more success.
