Privacy Policy

Privacy policies. They’re long, cumbersome, hard to read, and are generally ignored by the public at large. But, we’ve gotta have it, and since you’re here, we at the Marquette Tech District want to be as transparent as possible regarding the information collected by this website (http://www.marquettetech.com). So, let’s get through this as quickly and plainly as possible, shall we?

Email

We receive email at the info@marquettetech.com email address. The Marquette Tech District will use contact information provided to respond to any questions or comments directed to us in emails received. If we’re feeling conscientious and our memory is good, we may also send other resources to the contact info provided, if we think the resources would be helpful or otherwise worthwhile to a particular party. However, this is the extent to which this information will be used, and it will not be shared with any other parties without the expressed consent of the information provider.

Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager

The Marquette Tech District website uses Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to collect tracking and reporting information on our website traffic. This information is used solely to analyze from where and when the website receives traffic, and how we can adjust our marketing efforts to improve traffic. No personally identifiable information is collected by Google Analytics, and this information will not be shared with any other parties. However, if you do not wish for your data to be used by Google Analytics, we recommend using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Security

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.

Links

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, or wish for your information to be removed from our records, contact us via email at director@codefiworks.com.
