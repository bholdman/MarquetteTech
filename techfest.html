Marquette Techfest

A special opportunity to see the renovations of the historic Marquette Tech District and celebrate with the fast-growing startup and tech community.

SEE PHOTOS OF TECHFEST

Over 350 people came together last year. Don’t miss this one! Join us for this one-of-a-kind casual gala that celebrates the area’s tech and entrepreneurial community, supports downtown development, and recognizes Missouri’s best entrepreneurs.

Schedule of Events

Reception and Demo Day
6:00 p.m.
Meet aspiring tech entrepreneurs representing over 20 countries as they prepare to compete for $100,000 in cash and services and the chance to build their businesses with the support of the community in Cape Girardeau. Also meet youth and adults demonstrating their technology solutions from a hackathon for Big Brothers Big Sisters and code studio earlier in the week.
Ascent - Sneak Peak
7:00 p.m.
On the way the rooftop, get a sneak peek at the new home of the expanded Codefi coworking tech business center and coolest tech-powered office environment around.
Program
7:30 p.m.
  • Welcome from the Chairman of the Marquette Tech District Foundation, Inc.
  • Special Announcements and Presentations
After Party
8:30 p.m.
Descend to the lobby or stay on the rooftop to meet special guests, international startup competitors and continue celebrating the regions tech and entrepreneurial community.
