Marquette Techfest
  • Schedule of Events

An action-packed, day-long festival that brings together the talent, programs, and supporters of the technology and startup ecosystem in our region, culminating in the Marquette TechFest Gala.

Tickets on sale and special guests will be revealed June 1, T-minus:

This one-of-a-kind casual gala celebrates the area’s tech and entrepreneurial community, supports downtown development, and recognizes Missouri’s best entrepreneurs.

Schedule of Events

1ST50K Pitch Day
9:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m.
Aspiring tech entrepreneurs representing over 20 countries are in town competing for $50,000 cash, and the chance to build their business with the support of Southeast Missouri.
Fireside Chats at Codefi
12:00 - 1:00 p.m.
Exclusive events featuring successful founders, innovators, and influential individuals from around the world who share lessons learned on the road to building great companies. Past chats have included Glenn Campbell, Founder of LIDS; Mike Van de Ven, COO of Southwest Airlines; and Gabe Lozano, Founder and CEO of Lockerdome.
Youth Computer Science Fair
4:00 - 7:00 p.m.
Youth from around the region showcase what they have been working on during summer coding camps.
Marquette TechFest Gala
6:00 - 9:00 p.m.
Meet 1ST50K finalists and winner(s) are announced, check out the youth computer science fair projects, and hackathon solutions are showcased and winners selected. Program includes food, adult beverages, and a special guest speaker.
After Party
9:00 - 11:00 p.m.
Meet and greet with special guest. And because we like to party.
