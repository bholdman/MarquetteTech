Join The Celebration

Join us for this one-of-a-kind casual gala that celebrates the area’s tech and entrepreneurial community, supports downtown development, and recognizes Missouri’s best entrepreneurs. Be the first to party from the rooftop!

Schedule of Events

Reception and Demo Day
6:00 p.m.
Meet aspiring tech entrepreneurs representing over 20 countries as they prepare to compete for $100,000 in cash and services and the chance to build their businesses with the support of the community in Cape Girardeau. Also meet youth and adults demonstrating their technology solutions from a civic hackathon and code studio earlier in the week.
Ascent - Sneak Peak
7:00 p.m.
On the way the rooftop, get a sneak peek at the new Maker Space on the 2nd floor - a new lab for technology developers and entrepreneurs to create prototypes and assemble and test, software and hardware solutions, and check out the 6th floor - the new home of the expanded Codefi coworking tech business center and coolest tech-powered office environment around.
Program
7:30 p.m.
  • Welcome from the Chairman of the Marquette Tech District Foundation, Inc.
  • Special Announcements and Presentations
Lighting of the Building
8:00 p.m.
Join Jerry Howe, CEO of Big River Communications as he flips the switch that lights up the Marquette Tower with Gigabit internet service, marking completion of the first phase of the project to create a fiberhood and broadband public wi-fi network in downtown Cape Girardeau.
After Party
8:30 p.m.
Descend to the lobby or stay on the rooftop to meet special guests, international startup competitors and continue celebrating the regions tech and entrepreneurial community.
