Marquette TechWeek

Marquette TechWeek is a weeklong festival featuring the 1ST50K Startup Competition, Cape Girardeau Hackathon, TechFest and other networking events, Code Studio for kids, and more!

Itinerary

July 22-24

Code for Cape Hackathon

This civic hackathon is an event where developers, designers, artists, students, researchers, and civic leaders come together to design and develop tangible solutions to the problems we face daily in the towns we live. Teams with the best solutions win prizes and are eligible for service contracts to fully implement their technology solutions.

July 23

CodeCape Youth Code Studio

Youth Grades 3-8 will learn to create programs to solve problems and develop interactive games or stories they can share using Flappy Bird and other popular games. Students will be on hand at TechFest to demo their projects.

July 26

Tech Talk/Meetup

A sharing series where great minds in programming and innovation talk about what they know best. Think of it as TED for geeks. Join us at Broadway Biergarten afterwards for an opportunity to meet investors, mentors, employers, and like-minded techies and innovators.

July 27

1 Million Cups

A free national event designed to educate, engage, and connect entrepreneurs. Founders of Cape Crucible will present on their startup, a new maker space in the new Marquette Tower.

July 28

TechFest

Join us from 6 to 9 p.m. Thursday, July 28, 2016, at the iconic Marquette Tower (338 Broadway) to celebrate the region’s tech and entrepreneurial community, support non-profit groups and recognize Missouri’s best entrepreneurs.

July 29

1ST50K Startup Competition Pitch Day

Aspiring tech entrepreneurs representing over 20 countries prepare to compete for $100,000 in cash and services and the chance to build their businesses with the support of southeast Missouri. (private event)
