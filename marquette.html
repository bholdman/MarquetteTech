Marquette Tower

The epicenter of the Marquette Tech District is the iconic Marquette Tower. Anchored by an expansion of Codefi, the historic building offers small offices, suites, and coworking spaces to technology-based and innovative companies, and organizations that support them.

The building is wired with gigabit internet powered by a new state-of-the art fiber optic network that delivers the fastest speeds in the world to tenants in the building at NO ADDITIONAL COST. If that’s not enough, the Marquette Tower is home to a coffee shop and cafe offering hand-crafted coffee and outstanding breakfast and lunch menus, as well as an absolutely amazing rooftop cocktail lounge offering tapas and other light fare you can enjoy from the best dining view in southeast Missouri.

If you are interested in scheduling a tour to view the limited remaining space available, please contact us.
