Impact

Downtown Economic Development

Thanks to dedicated leaders and successful entrepreneurs, we’re poised to take another big step forward. The Marquette Tech District is the next big step in making downtown Cape Girardeau a great place to live, work, and play. Substantial economic impact will be generated by the addition of 200-300 talented, enterprising professionals who spend their days downtown, and attraction of guests who value a digitally connected downtown experience and the business they will bring to local small businesses.

Tech Startup Economy

Research indicates that most net new jobs are created by companies less than five years old, and the technology communications sector is leading global growth. Our local technology sector is emerging and needs density and infrastructure to grow. The Marquette Tech District is a creative effort to attract, nurture, and promote Cape Girardeau’s tech economy through a combination of technology-enabled initiatives and business incentives, private business support and member-driven programming. In just the last year, Codefi and its affiliates have created 12 technology-based startups that have attracted over $1.2 million in private investments.
